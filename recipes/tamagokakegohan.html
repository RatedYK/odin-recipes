TamagoKakeGohan

An staple japanese dish that is simple and takes less than 5 minutes to make whilst also delighting ones tastebuds

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 100g Cooked Rice
  • Tsp of Soy Sauce
  1. Put the cooked rice into a serving bowl
  2. Crack and add the raw egg onto the rice
  3. Add the tablespoon of soy sauce
  4. Stir Well
